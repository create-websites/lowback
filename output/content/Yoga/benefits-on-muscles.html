Mental Benefits of Yoga
These days the lifestyle of people has changed. The amount of physical activities has reduced and spent more time sitting in front of laptop/computers which has become the main cause for back and neck pain.To lessen these pains ,yoga is very essential. Though it might require a little effort at initial stages but later on it help you in long term.
Yoga makes your body active and flexible. It also provides can provide several healing benefits. More benefits of Yoga are:
Healing injured back muscles
Speeding time to recover from an injury
Strengthens muscles to prevent re-injury
Active and flexible body

Physical Benefits of Yoga
Strengthening Muscles – You can increase strength in the muscles with the help of holding positions in Yoga. These holding positions are not at all uncomfortable but they require concentration which in turn strengthens very muscles and muscle groups. Regular yoga can help in lessening pain and can also eliminate pain.
Stretching and relaxation- Yoga not only strengthens the muscles but also help in stretching and relaxation. It reduces tension in stress-carrying muscles. While doing Yoga it is very important to keep in mind the breathing pattern. It is advisable to hold the breath while holding a position and release breathe while releasing holding position.
Posture balance - Consistent practice of yoga helps in improving body alignment and posture. Proper body posture helps to maintain the natural curvature of the spine, which in turn helps in reducing lower back pain.
Some poses for relieving back pain are Pigeon Pose, Downward-Facing Dog, Child's Pose, Triangle Pose, Cat and Cow Pose, Upward-Facing Dog , Bridge (Setu Bandha Sarvangasana)etc. But is advisable to visit a doctor before starting these types of exercises as he can suggest poses as per the amount of pain you are facing.
