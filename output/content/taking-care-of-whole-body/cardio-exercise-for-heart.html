3. The Best Approach: Polarized Training

Research indicates that the most effective strategy for achieving goals related to health, longevity, or performance is Polarized Training, often called the 80/20 rule.

80

% Low Intensity (Zone 2)

Constructs the 'engine.' Enhances capillary density and mitochondrial function while preventing the build-up of stress hormones, promoting sustained performance.

20

% High Intensity (Zone 4/5)

Sharpen the 'blade' to challenge your heart pumping capacity and fast-twitch muscle fibers with intense effort.

How to measure without tech?

Being able to communicate in full sentences but lacking the ability to sing puts you in the ideal position (Zone 2).
